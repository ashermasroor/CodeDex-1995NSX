The First gen Honda NSX is my dream car especially the 1995 NSX-T edition. My earliest memory of cars comes from playing the Demo version of Need for Speed Underground 2 on my old windows XP computer. Another Core memory relating to my love for cars is whenever I would get sad by anything. My mom would give me a warm, make me a warm bowl of instant noodles and playing the PC Knight Rider game as entertainment.From that point i've loved KITT's car.

My first time seeing the 1st Gen Honda NSX instantly fired a neuron in my brain connecting the super sharp front bumper shape of KITT's car and the NSX and i've since liked the 1st Gen Honda NSX. As i've grown older and learnt more about cars and developed a preference for the JDM Car Market. Explored JDMs and played racing games. My love for the 1995 NSX has only grown more and more and soon enough the 1st Gen Honda NSX became the car of my dreams.
