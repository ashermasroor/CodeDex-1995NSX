Engine and Performance

Engine Type: 3.0-liter V6, DOHC, 24-valve

Power Output:

Manual Transmission: 270 hp @ 7100 rpm

Transmission:

Standard: 5-speed manual

Optional: 4-speed automatic with SportShift mode

Acceleration (0-60 mph): Approximately 5.6 seconds

Top Speed: 167.8 mph (270 km/h)

Length: 4425 mm (174.2 in)

Height: 1175 mm (46.3 in)

Wheelbase: 2530 mm (99.6 in)

Manual: Approximately 3141 lbs (1425 kg)
